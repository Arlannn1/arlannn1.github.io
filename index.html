Bio & Research Interests

I am currently a fourth year PhD student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Professor Irwin King. I received my B.S. degree from Nanjing University in 2016.

My research interests are machine learning and data mining for information retrieval with applications of search & recommender systems. My recent working topics include but are not limited to:
  • quantization methods for efficient neural networks;
  • graph-based recommender systems and enhancing techniques, e.g., social regularization, knowledge graphs.
  • neural ranking models with natural language understanding;
    • I am also interested in traditional graph mining problems such as community retrieval problems.
    Feel free to contact me if you are seeking any form of collaboration.
